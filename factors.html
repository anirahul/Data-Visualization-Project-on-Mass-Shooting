Reflections

For the contributing factors chart each iteration of a “yes” is logged as a yes, and a no is still no. When looking at a list of contributing factors a few things are clearly present; but also a few things stand out as out of the ordinary. One being clear is that “signs of crisis” comes out over all at the highest amount of yes’s, whereas “violent history” is out passed by “social media posts related to shootings.” The second highest, which many would have mistaken for the first highest would be suicidality, the intent of the shooter was to die in the course of the shooting, or to take their own life upon completion.
